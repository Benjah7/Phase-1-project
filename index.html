About

Cranium Studio, is a versatile & multi-disciplinary Photography Studio, Events Venue and Art Gallery in the heart of Nairobi. A space created to showcase a multitude of creative forms of expression, give creative practitioners a platform to interact but above all a space that welcomes all to learn, engage and be inspired... with emphasis on photographic forms of expression. At Cranium Studio we welcome creative practitioners from all over the world. Additionally, we look forward to working closely with all manner of creative practitioners within Kenya and as well as Africa. Our aim is the continual support of local creative talent, creating a larger, wider platform for photographers as well as artists in general, in the hope of contributing to the growing recognition of Kenya's large Art & developing Photography culture
